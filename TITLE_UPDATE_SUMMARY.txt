RESEARCH SUMMARY
================================================================================

TITLE: Gaussian Quantum Steering and Entanglement Dynamics in Coupled Lossy 
       Waveguides: Hierarchy and Robustness Analysis

SCOPE: This MS thesis extends foundational work on quantum entanglement in 
       coupled lossy waveguides by introducing comprehensive analysis of 
       Gaussian quantum steering, emphasizing:

       1. STEERING vs ENTANGLEMENT distinction (asymmetric vs symmetric)
       2. HIERARCHY of quantum correlations 
          (Discord ⊇ Entanglement ⊇ Steering ⊇ Bell Nonlocality)
       3. DYNAMICS of correlations over propagation distance
       4. ROBUSTNESS under realistic material loss

SYSTEM: Two-mode coupled optical waveguides with:
        - Evanescent coupling (strength J)
        - Material loss (decay rate γ)
        - Various input states (photon number, NOON, squeezed)
        - Realistic parameters (γ/J from 1/50 to 1/7)

METHODS: Covariance matrix analysis, logarithmic negativity, steering criteria,
         Lindblad master equation, numerical simulation
